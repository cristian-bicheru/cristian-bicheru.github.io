What Is This Site?

This site is dedicated to my Who Am I summative project, it will contain lots of information about me. This includes my Myers-Briggs personality type, my learning styles, etc.

Character Quiz

For the character quiz, I got "Tweety". This means that I am loyal and a 'best friend', that I'm witty and calm most of the time, and that people can really count on me. This can help me in school because knowing how other people see me can help me work more efficiently in groups.

Learning Styles

The learning styles quiz said that I am a 43% visual learner, 29% auditory learner, and 28% kinesthetic learner. This means that I like to take detailed notes, I like to sit near the front, and I like to turn concepts into charts and diagrams. In order to learn and retain information better in school I should: underline or highlight key ideas in my notes, make charts and diagrams, and use graphic organizers.

True Colors

The true colors quiz said that my true color is green. Green people are 'thinkers'. They are self sufficient and independant, rely on logic, and do research/think before making decisions. This will help me in school since knowing more about my personality will help me in group projects or assignments.

Myers-Briggs Personality

The Myers-Briggs personailty test said that my personality code is INTP. This stands for introverted, intuiting, thinking, and percieving. Some key descriptions of my personality are that I prefer to work in smaller groups, I use intuition when completing tasks, and that I am fair and use my brain when making decisions. In general, knowing my own personality can help me maximize my contributions when working in a group setting.

Multiple Intelligences

The multiple intelligences quiz said that I have strong logic/math skills, intrapersonal skills, and kinesthetic skills. This means that I enjoy exploring relations between things, understanding how things work, it also means I have a good sense of myself, and that I like to think things over and spend time by myself. Additionally, it means I like to move and might be good at sports. This can help me a lot in school since it can help me choose a job field and improve my learning ability.

Intrests

According to the interests quiz, my interest type is "The Investigator". This means that I enjoy investigative-style jobs. Investigators are typically inquisitive, organized, and have a knack for observing fine details. According to the quiz, when you need a detailed, systematic analysis with well-reasoned conclusions, ask an investigator! I think this description does suit me well. I think I would enjoy jobs such as being a data scientist, programmer, or researcher. This knowledge can help me choose the right career path for my future.

Knowledge

The knowledge quiz said that I'm skilled in Business, Computers, and Mathematics (top 3). This means that I am most skilled/interested in those particular fields. Knowing this will be helpful since it will help influence which career path I will take.

Motivations

From the motivations quiz, I got that my primary motivation factor is achievement, and my second motivation factor is independance. This means that I'm driven by achievement in my everyday life, and I enjoy doing things alone. I guess I agree with the first part, but the second part is only true sometimes. For small projects, I don't like working in teams since it's inefficient, however in larger projects, teamwork helps a ton. Knowing my motivations is extremely important since it will let me make goals which are easier to achieve (by making it so that I have more motivation to complete them).

Transferable Skills

From the transferable skills handout, I got that I have strong problem solving and leadership skills. I've become an executive for two clubs this year, mathletes and computer science. This demonstrates that I enjoy and have skills in leadership. I also thoroughly enjoy and practice math and computer science. These fields require lots of problem solving, which has developed my problem solving skillset. Knowing my skills will help me make the most of my time in high school, by working on my strengths and improving them.
